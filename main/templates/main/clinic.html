    Important information about our project


    UC description There are four roles: patient, receptionist, doctor and administrator. The patient logs into his personal account by entering a login and password, after they have access to the functions of viewing pages with a medical record and making an appointment. Optionally, when viewing records in the medical record, every patient can select a specific doctor. On the Appointment Review page, they can cancel an existing appointment, freeing up a window in the doctor's appointment schedule, or they can make a new appointment. When registering, patients must select the corps, specialist, date and time of admission. Additionally, a choice of the method of sending a coupon and a reminder of the reception is implemented. The receptionist logs into the system by entering a login and password, after they have access to the functions of viewing pages with the schedule of all doctors and recording patients for appointments. If desired, when viewing the schedule, they can select a specialty and a specific doctor. On the Appointment View page, they can cancel an existing appointment, freeing up a window in the doctor's appointment schedule, or they can schedule a patient for a new appointment. When registering, receptionists must select a specialist, date and time of admission. Additionally, a choice of the method of sending a coupon and a reminder of the reception is implemented. The doctor logs into his personal account by entering his login and password, and they have access to the functions of viewing pages with the schedule of all doctors, recording patients for appointments and editing the patient's medical record. When viewing the schedule, they can select a specialty and a specific doctor. On the Review Appointment page, they can cancel an existing appointment to free up a window in your appointment schedule, or they can schedule a patient for a new appointment. When registering, doctors must select the date, time of reception, as well as the method of sending the coupon and reminder of the appointment. Editing a medical record involves entering new data about the patient's health status. The administrator has expanded functionality: additionally implemented the ability to edit data, add new users to the system or delete their information.
